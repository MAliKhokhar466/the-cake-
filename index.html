A Little About Cake History

People have been gathering around cakes for centuries—starting back when they were basically sweet, honey-soaked breads! Today, we're keeping that tradition alive. We built this space to make sharing those sweet moments easier, delivering fresh baked goods right to your door.

Our Story & The Team

How It Started

We’re just a group of passionate friends who realized that life is too short for bad cake. What started as messy late-night kitchen experiments turned into a genuine love for baking and sharing.

Our Promise

We don't believe in cutting corners. If we wouldn't serve it to our own families, we won't serve it to you. Fresh eggs, real butter, and zero artificial nonsense.
